Welcome to Nefertiti- Querencia

A Subsidiary of Vision Africa Group

At Nefertiti Querencia, we are more than just a distributor of beauty products we are curators of African elegance and style.
As a proud subsidiary of Vision Africa Group, we offer a range of premium beauty and fashion items, from cosmetics to human hair and traditional African apparel.
By keeping close ties with Africa’s top fashion designers, we ensure that every piece we provide reflects the vibrant, evolving world of African fashion.
Whether you’re attending a special event or celebrating a personal milestone, our goal is to make your moments unforgettable.

About Nefetiti

At Nefetiti, we believe in empowering individuals to express their unique beauty. Founded by a passionate group of beauty enthusiasts, our mission is to provide high-quality beauty products that cater to diverse needs and preferences.

We meticulously curate our product offerings, ensuring that each item meets our high standards for quality, safety, and effectiveness. Our commitment to sustainability drives us to source ingredients responsibly and package our products thoughtfully.
