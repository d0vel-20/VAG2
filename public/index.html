Our Mission at Vision Africa

At Vision Africa, we specialize in connecting foreign manufacturers with local African retailers, partners, and distributors of cutting-edge electronics. Our mission is to empower local businesses by providing access to a diverse range of high-quality products that enhance everyday life and drive economic growth.

Why Choose VA?

VA helps your business grow faster and smarter by providing a platform that allows you to communicate and do business directly with distributors and retail buyers worldwide. Here's why VA is the perfect partner for your business needs:

Who Needs VA?

If your business is ready to enter new markets, VA helps you achieve your goals faster and smarter. Our platform offers a unique environment for communicating and doing business with distributors and retail buyers worldwide.

Why Use VA?

Finding a new partner can take months or even years. VA reduces your search time by 95%, finding the right business partner faster and more cost-efficiently.

Does VA Sell Products?

VA does not sell products directly. Instead, we provide an exclusive platform where you showcase your products to distributors and retail buyers and initiate conversations directly.

Does VA Take Commission?

VA does not take any commission on your orders. However, we offer a payment protection solution for a small administrative fee, which you can opt into at checkout.

How Much to Join?

You can sign up to VA for free to showcase your products. If you wish to contact distributors, you will need to upgrade to one of the paid plans. For more information, check the pricing page.

Discount on Advertising?

VA offers a cost-effective solution for connecting with quality distributors. Unlike trade shows or agencies that are time-consuming and expensive, VA connects you with vetted distributors at a fraction of the cost.

Expedited Services

With our Executive Search package, VA offers a custom solution. Our team helps you find the right buyers for your product in your target markets and connects you directly with them.

Global Connections

We partner with reputable manufacturers from around the world, ensuring that our clients receive only the best products.

Streamlined Logistics

We handle all aspects of the supply chain, from sourcing to delivery, making it easier for you to focus on growing your business.
