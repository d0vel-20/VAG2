About Us

Learn more about who we are and our commitment to providing the best electronics.

Our Mission

At Vision Africa, we specialize in connecting foreign manufacturers with local African retailers, partners, and distributors of cutting-edge electronics. Our mission is to empower local businesses by providing access to a diverse range of high-quality products that enhance everyday life and drive economic growth.

Who are we

Headquartered in Lagos with an additional office in Owerri, Eastern Nigeria, Vision Africa has been at the forefront of providing innovative, reliable, and top-tier products to meet the diverse needs of our clients across the country. With over a decade of experience, we have built a reputation for delivering excellence in both the products we distribute and the relationships we maintain with our partners and customers.

Our Products

We specialize in the distribution of high-performance electronics and electrical appliances, sourced from world-renowned manufacturers. Some of our flagship products include:
MGNationale Air Conditioners: Premium quality air conditioners from leading Chinese manufacturers such as Shenbao Aircondition and Ken Lights Limited.
SmartEye Surveillance Cameras: Advanced surveillance solutions from PKB World in Japan, designed to provide top-tier security for homes and businesses.
Our focus on quality ensures that every product we distribute meets rigorous standards, providing both reliability and satisfaction to our end-users.

Entertainment Industry Expertise

In addition to our role as a distributor of electronics, Vision Africa is deeply involved in Nigeria's thriving entertainment industry. Over the years, we have leveraged our presence in the industry, utilizing music, advertisements, and social media to enhance our brand's visibility and connect more efficiently with our customers. By combining our expertise in distribution with modern marketing strategies, we ensure that our products reach a wide audience, achieving seamless sales and distribution across Nigeria.
